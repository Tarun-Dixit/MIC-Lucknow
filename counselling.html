With more and more people being affected directly or indirectly by COVID-19, there is a need to provide psychological first aid to relieve their distress and prevent them from developing more serious mental health issues and to optimize their mental health.

Our counselors understand and address the mental health needs of the individuals, who are awaiting results of COVID-19 tests, confirmed COVID-19 individuals, health care workers working in COVID hospitals and their family members. The counselors can aid these individuals in getting reliable information and alleviate their distress.

Goals of counselling-

  1. Encouraging safety, health and hygiene
  2. Reducing immediate distress
  3. Take constructive steps towards solving life problems
  4. Help individuals cope better with their life challenges
  5. Generate a sense of realistic hope
